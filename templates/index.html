Historical Chat With a Hint of Modern Aspects


The following example is an approximation of what a conversation between Socrates and Dante (You) would have looked like!

The chat is a combination of modern and historical aspects, The chat bot was trained using >1000 lines of dialogue and is capable of responding to a variety of intents. The premise of the bot is to have a conversation with Socrates, and to learn about his life and philosophy. It manages to do this by using a variety of intents, and by using a variety of responses to each intent. Each data point (line of dialogue) was classified and given a specific subject which are then converted to intents.

To interact with 'Socrates' simply choose a subject from the menu and the bot will respond accordingly.
